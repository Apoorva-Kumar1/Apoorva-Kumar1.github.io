About us

ChessMates Club

Chessmates, the official chess club of Delhi Public School, Dwarka, is a thriving community of young chess enthusiasts. Founded in 2022 by Priyansh Gehani, the club has become a cornerstone of the school's co-curricular life and one of the most popular clubs in our school with the highest participation.

The club's notable achievements include securing second place in an international FIDE-rated rapid chess tournament, showcasing the talent and determination of its players. In 2025, Chessmates launched DPS Dwarka's first student-authored chess newspaper, providing a platform for members to express their insights and creativity.
